AlertGenerator(class)
Acts as the system's brain, using a Map<String, double> to store
personalized thresholds for different health metrics. This allows the system to compare 
incoming vitals against the limits of the patients in real time. The dependency on the 
Alert class ensures that an alert object is only instantiated when a specific threshold 
is triggered, maintaining an efficient workflow.

Alert(class)
The Alert class' main idea is to transfer data. It is designed to package all critical 
information (patientID, condition, timeStamp and urgencyLevel) into a single unit.
This design ensures that medical staff receive a complete context of the emergency. By
separating the alert data from the generation logic, the system remains scalable and can
process high volumes of events without losing metadata.

AlertManager(class) & MedicalStaff(class)
Handles the distribution and routing of alerts to the appropriate staff.
By maintaining a staffRegistry, the manager can target specific individuals or roles
based on the alert's nature. The 1 to 1..n multiplicity shows that one central manager
can coordinate with multiple staff members, ensuring that every triggered alert is reliably
delivered to a person.
