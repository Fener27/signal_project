PatientIdentifier(class)
Designed to be the primary point of contact for incoming data streams. Its goal is to provide a lookup service
that translates raw IDs into HospitalPatient objects. By including a resolveId method, the system ensures that
no data point remains anonymous. This fulfills the requirement for real time patient matching.

HospitalPatient(class)
Acts as the source of truth. Basically, the class includes history and verificationStatus to provide the medical
staff with context(not only the name of the patient). This allows the system to provide the historical trends
necessary for informed clinical analysis.

IdentityManager(class)
Included as the system's failsafe. The class' design is to handle mismatches and anomalies. By doing this
into a class, the system can handle cases where a patient is not found in the database without crashing
the data stream, logging the error for administrative review instead.
